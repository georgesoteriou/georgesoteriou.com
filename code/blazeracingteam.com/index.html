WHAT WE DO / FORMULA1 IN SCHOOLS

We are Blaze Racing Team. The team consists of six students from The English School, Nicosia. We represent Cyprus in the Formula 1 in Schools World Finals 2016.

The competition is organized annually by the Formula One in Schools Ltd, a non-profit company. This competition challenges teams of students to race against the clock and deploy their skills to prepare a business plan, develop a budget, collect sponsorships and finally to design and manufacture a scale model of an F1 car. This inspires the students to use IT and learn about physics, aerodynamics, design, marketing, media skills as well as teamwork. The teams compete against each other, putting their cars to test, first on a national level and then on an international level. Each team will be judged on the basis of their performance throughout the course of time. This year Texas, USA is set to host the F1 in Schools World Finals in October 2016.
